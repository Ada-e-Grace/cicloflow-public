1. Who we are

CicloFlow is an app focused on menstrual health education and cycle tracking, designed to help you better understand your phases, symptoms, and well-being throughout the month. This Privacy Policy explains, in clear language, how your data is handled inside the app.

2. What data we process

To work, CicloFlow only uses the data needed to calculate and track your menstrual cycle. In particular:

  • Basic identification data: name or nickname you choose to provide.
  • Cycle data: last period date, average cycle length, average period length.
  • Symptom and mood records: when you choose to log how you feel and which symptoms appear throughout the cycle.
  • Local preferences: notification settings, reminders, and notices within the app.

This cycle, symptom, and mood information is considered sensitive personal data under LGPD because it is related to your health.

3. Where and how your data is stored

All data you log in CicloFlow is stored only on this device. The app uses a local database and local system preferences to keep your information.

This means:

  • We do not send your data to external servers or to the cloud.
  • We do not automatically back up your data to third-party services.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or selling information.

Note about system backups: even though CicloFlow works offline, the data stored on this device may be automatically included in iCloud (iOS) or Google (Android) backups if you have those backups enabled in your device settings. Those backups are managed by Apple or Google and are governed by their respective privacy policies. You can disable or remove them directly in your operating system settings.

4. Why we use your data (purposes)

The data you log is used exclusively to:

  • Calculate and display your menstrual cycle phases visually.
  • Help identify symptom patterns and mood variations throughout the cycle.
  • Offer educational content and self-care suggestions appropriate to each phase.
  • Schedule optional local notifications (such as daily reminders, luteal-phase alerts, or ongoing PMDD notices) if you choose to enable them.
  • Generate personalized tips and advice based on the symptoms you log.

You can enable or disable CicloFlow notifications at any time, both in the app's own settings and in your operating system's notification settings (iOS or Android).

We do not use this data to create commercial profiles, send advertising, or share your information with third parties.

5. Legal basis for data processing (LGPD)

Because CicloFlow handles sensitive personal data (related to your health), the main legal basis used is your consent, given freely, clearly, and explicitly, in accordance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (LGPD).

By continuing onboarding and using the app, you agree that CicloFlow processes the informed data for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and on the onboarding consent screen.

6. Data sharing

CicloFlow does not share your health data with third parties.

There is no data transfer to external analytics services, ads, social networks, or commercial partners. Everything you log is kept only on this device for use within the app itself.

The only exception is payment processing when you subscribe to Premium, which is handled by Google Play or the App Store. See section 12 for details.

Note about exported data: when you use the app's export feature to share a report (for example, with your doctor or by email), the file leaves the app and stops being protected by CicloFlow. From that point on, it is your responsibility to store, send, and delete that file safely.

7. How long we keep your data

Your data remains stored on this device while the app is installed and while you do not choose to delete it.

You can, at any time:

  • Use the "Delete all my data from this device" option inside Settings / Account; and/or
  • Uninstall the app, which also removes the local database from this device.

After deletion, data cannot be recovered by the app.

8. Your rights as a data subject

According to LGPD, you have the right to:

  • Access the information you logged in the app.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated data (for example, edit your cycle information).
  • Revoke consent for data processing by deleting your information in the app or uninstalling CicloFlow from this device.
  • Request more clarification about how your data is handled.

In CicloFlow, many of these rights can be exercised directly in app screens, especially in "Settings / Account" and in data editing forms.

9. Consent revocation and data deletion

You can revoke your consent and stop the use of your sensitive data at any time in two main ways:

  • Inside the app: go to "Settings / Account" and use "Delete all my data from this device".
  • Outside the app: uninstall CicloFlow from your device.

In both cases, locally stored data is no longer accessible by the app on this device.

10. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect app improvements or legal changes. When that happens, the latest version will be available on this screen with the last update date.

We recommend reviewing this content periodically to stay informed about how your data is handled.

12. Payments and Premium subscription

CicloFlow offers an optional Premium subscription, processed through Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). Here is what that means for your data:

  • Payment data (card, address, store account identifiers) is handled exclusively by Google or Apple under their own privacy policies. CicloFlow does not receive or store card data.
  • What the app stores locally about your subscription: status (active or not), product ID (monthly/annual), and expected expiration date. This information is used only to unlock Premium features on this device.
  • Restoring purchases: when you tap "Restore purchases", the app queries the store about subscriptions linked to your Google or Apple account — no information leaves the app any other way.
  • Validity check: confirmation that the subscription is active is done by the store's own billing system using cryptographic tokens. CicloFlow does not send these tokens to any external server.
